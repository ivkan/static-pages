Frequently Asked Questions

What is Immerss Comprehensive Clienteling and how does it work?

Immerss is a 1-1 virtual co-shopping platform that enables online shoppers to connect directly with a sales associate, product expert, or brand ambassador through video, voice, or chat. The platform allows associates to guide customers through product recommendations, sizing advice, and purchase decisions in real-time, offering a personalized shopping experience.

How does Immerss integrate with my existing e-commerce platform?

Immerss is designed to integrate seamlessly with most e-commerce platforms. It works through a simple widget or link embedded on your product pages, allowing customers to initiate a connection with a sales associate easily. Our team provides support to ensure smooth integration and minimal disruption to your workflow.

Can I customize the co-shopping experience to match my brand?

Yes, Immerss allows for customization to match your brand's look and feel. You can tailor the interface, messaging, and even the types of support offered to ensure that the experience aligns with your brand's values and customer service goals.

What types of products are best suited for Immerss?

Immerss is ideal for merchants selling higher consideration products that benefit from personalized guidance, such as fashion, beauty, electronics, furniture, and luxury goods. It works best for products where fit, features, or quality are important and where shoppers may have more questions before making a purchase.

What kind of devices can the tool be used on?

The Immerss Digital Clienteling Tool is available on both iOS (iPhone) and desktop platforms. This flexibility allows your team to use the tool from their mobile devices while on the sales floor or from a computer when managing customer interactions in a more comprehensive, desktop environment.

What is Live Event Shopping?

Live Event Shopping is a new way for merchants to engage with customers in real time through live streaming. During these events, businesses can showcase products, answer questions, and offer exclusive deals, all while viewers can shop directly from the stream. It combines the excitement of live video with the convenience of e-commerce, providing an immersive shopping experience.

How does Immerss Live Event Shopping work?

With Immerss, merchants can easily set up and host live shopping events. Simply select your products, set up your stream, and go live! Viewers will be able to watch your event, interact with you via live chat, and purchase products in real time without leaving the stream. Product links are tagged within the livestream, so customers can click and buy directly, creating a seamless shopping experience.

Immerss automatically scrapes the relevant product information from your website, including descriptions, pricing, features, sizes, and common FAQs.
